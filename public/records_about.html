What is "The Hunter's Records"?


A website that allows users to find monsters and weapons that from the game Monster Hunter World!

Monster Hunter can be hard to get into, but "The Hunter's Records" lets you find out more about any monster or weapon from Monster Hunter World! Instructions on how to get started can be found on the search page.

Information about the Creator

I'm Shahil Paudel, and just like you, I wanted to learn more about the monsters and weapons in Monster Hunter World in a simple and easy-to-navigate way. So I went ahead and made it myself!
